Application Development

Custom software built from first principles — not templated off-the-shelf solutions with your logo dropped in.

IT & Process Consulting

Strategic advisory with a practitioner's perspective.

Many organizations invest in technology without a coherent strategy — accumulating systems that don't integrate, duplicate effort, and drive up operational cost. We assess your current state, identify high-value optimization opportunities, and design a practical roadmap for improvement.

  • Technology landscape assessments
  • Business process mapping & re-engineering
  • Digital transformation roadmaps
  • IT governance and policy frameworks
  • Vendor evaluation and contract advisory

Database Administration

Your data infrastructure, managed with precision.

Poorly designed or under-maintained databases are a silent performance drain. We provide expert design, optimization, and ongoing administration for relational and non-relational systems, ensuring data integrity, availability, and security at every scale.

  • Database design & normalization
  • Query optimization & indexing strategy
  • Backup, recovery, and high availability
  • Data migration and ETL pipelines
  • PostgreSQL, MySQL, SQL Server, MongoDB
